Theme 4 - Learning Active Citizenship and Governance

In Theme 4 we will examine the learning process in becoming an active citizen. In particular we'll look at:

  • What are the main features of the learning processes in becoming an active citizen?

  • What are the factors that contribute to these learning processes?

  • What can be done to strengthen them?

We will explore these issues through a number of illustrative stories of active citizens and the organisational structures they are involved in. We also invite you to draw on your own experience as an active citizen or/and as an educator.
