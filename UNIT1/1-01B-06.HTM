Gerda's story

Gerda was raised in a Christian tradition, and a Catholic school. Her parents were always very conscious about the environment and participated in an environmental organisation. Gerda and her sister both participated in the youth department of that organisation. One could say that they were really socialised in environmental consciousness. She went to university to study agricultural engineering from the same orientation. Also the 'third world' is one of the themes she was interested in and concerned about. She went to Rwanda for a year and a half under an international development project, but wondered if that experience was not more to her benefit than it has been for Rwanda itself. A lot of her engagements or links with certain organisations happened through knowing people. This was the case for her commitment in a 'foodteam' (voedselteams), in an 'ecoteam', in the Green Party (Agalev), etc. Within her professional career, she chose jobs that are in line with her major interest, namely the environment. One can say that she tries to enact her commitment via her job. Her individual lifestyle represents her main interests.
