Active citizens - short stories

View a single story per page

Now we will read some short stories of a few active citizens . We will try to develop a framework which enables us to understand the diversity of active citizenship from the citizens' own points of view.

In our research we interviewed people in two age groups: between 25 - 40 and between 55 - 70. This was for reasons explained here. This means that all our examples fall into these two age groups. While you read the stories, you might want to consider whether their stories are different from those of people from other age groups?

Before reading individual stories you might want to look at some of the questions for reflection. You also can look at the questions while you read the stories. Alternatively you might want to take a printout of the questions in order to avoid switching between screens.
