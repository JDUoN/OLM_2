Dolores' story

Dolores has been working in the cloth manufacturing sector for a long time. For the last three years she has been teaching at a vocational training institute for women. While working as a teacher, she also started to pursue university studies, in Social Education, in order to acquire more knowledge about education and to improve herself in her job. Her studies increased her motivation to occupy herself with the problems many people face regarding their integration in the labour market, especially in the case of immigrants. Furthermore, she explains that she herself experienced being an immigrant in a foreign country, as she lived in London before Spain was part of the EU. She says this feeling motivated her to help immigrants to gain equal opportunities for education and employment. Currently she is unemployed and looking for a job. Because of this situation, with more free time available, her intention of doing something for her community has increased her disposition to do some voluntary work in socio-cultural organisations.
