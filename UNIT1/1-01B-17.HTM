Carlos' story

Carlos is an engineer who comes from an immigrant, working-class family. He has been brought up in solidarity values and in a commitment to the preservation of the environment. His social orientation and worries led him first to participate in the JOC (Young Working Christians) and later on to promote an organisation co-ordinating body (an organisation co-ordinator) in Rubí. Recently, an illness has made him to consume ecological and organic food only, which has connected him to a co-operative concerned with responsible consumption. This has led him to take a critical stance on environmental matters. At present, he is starting up a co-operative in Rubí that aims to achieve a widespread knowledge about this type of products. At the same time, he continues to be linked to the movement through the ACO (Catholic Working Action) and the organisation co-ordinator.
