Victor's story

Victor has been a local politician since 1999. He studied engineering at University and works as an IT salesperson for an international computer company but political activity takes up most of his spare time. After helping at the general election in 1990 he has helped with canvassing and attended political party conferences from the age of 18. His father was involved in politics in Greece and his family are all politically aware. He intends to stand for council again in 2003 and then might try to get into Parliament as an MP. Currently his support work for the Conservative Party takes up more time than his council work.
