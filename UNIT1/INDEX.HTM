Theme 1 - What is Active Citizenship and Governance?

In Theme 1 we are going to explore the following four issues:

  • What kinds of active citizenship and governance are there?

  • What are the main themes and issues which lead people to become active (such as the environment, road safety, urban issues, workplace-related)?

  • What are the different levels and contexts where people engage in active citizenship and governance (such as at home, local, national, European, global)?

  • Can we come to a common understanding of what 'active citizenship' and 'governance' mean?

We explore these issues by using the life stories of a number of active citizens from various parts of Europe. We also ask you to draw on your own experience as an active citizen and as an educator.
