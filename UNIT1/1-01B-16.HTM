Bor's story

Bor was born to a conservative family, his father is a miller, mother a secretary. He was influenced by his father, who inoculated values in him like being honest, fighting against injustice, and speaking out when you do not like something. He thinks that this kind of breeding makes him "such an annoying unionist". He decided to become a journalist when he was in the fifth grade of primary school. After finishing college he went to a faculty for social sciences and became a journalist at age of 25. He thinks he did not learn much at the faculty. During the faculty he married his high school love.

Bo is a father of two, with one on the way. He served in the army in the former Yugoslavia, just at the time of increasing tensions in the country. He attended the first democratic elections there. He started to work for the national TV when he was in the second year of college. He is still working there. He became a president of the trade union in 1998. He said that he is doing that because of the working collective. He added "I'm not going to make any compromises. The members are the union, not me!".

He is very busy so he would like to have more time for his family.
