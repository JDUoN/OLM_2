Godfrey's story

Godfrey is a retired development economist. He was born and raised in Argentina and came to England when he was 18 years old in order to see the world. He worked his way through universities and gained degrees in development economics. He lived and worked for international organisations in Zimbabwe, Zambia, Ethiopia and Chile, then in the UK for the Ministry of Overseas Development. He is the chairman of Age Concern in Guildford and has been a local councillor for 9 years. Prior to that he was chairman of the Guildford Society which is devoted to the protection of Guildford. He made the Society effective by bringing together well-qualified local people to challenge the Council on several issues. He fought elections three times before winning and thought he would be more effective as a politician than as an environmental campaigner. He derives satisfaction in being able to get things done and has introduced reforms. He says 'things are changing so someone must be changing them so why shouldn't it be me'.
