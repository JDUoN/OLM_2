Some questions for Reflection

  • Why did he/she become an active citizen? What are the main issues and concerns that matter to him/her?
  • What are the different areas of life in which he/she is an active citizen?
  • In what capacity is he/she active? (e.g., educator, organiser, fund raiser, etc.)
  • How does his/her gender, sexuality, age, etc., influence/affect what he/she does?
  • What can you learn from this story about how you could facilitate/stimulate active citizenship? What kind of support would be required?

Jot down some notes based on the above questions and anything else that you think is important, and join the discussion with your fellow participants.
