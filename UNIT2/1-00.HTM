Overview

Who are Active Citizens?

An educator who is working with a large voluntary sector organisation tried to answer this question. He began by saying that 'I wish I were a firefighter! Then it would be easier for me to explain what I am doing and who I am working with.' For him it was difficult to give a straightforward answer, about who an active citizen is.

Let's pause for a moment ...

Reflection

Imagine you have just bumped into a friend you haven't been in touch with for a long time. You tell him/her what your job is, or what you do as a voluntary activity, and your involvement with active citizens. Your friend asks you 'Who is an active citizen?'

What would your answer be? How would you describe an active citizen? What kinds of words would you use?

Take about 2 minutes to think about the kind of people you are working with, and try to jot down a few key words that come to your mind.

In this theme, we are going to explore this question and come up with some criteria to describe and appreciate the diversity of active citizens. We will consider the diverse backgrounds of active citizens and look at the different experiences they undergo in becoming active citizens. We will also look at the variety of roles they play as active citizens, and try to understand any common characteristics (if there are any) that can be found among active citizens.

Discussion forum

In the above activity, you may have reflected upon the question 'Who are active citizens?', and jotted down some thoughts.

Post your thoughts on the Discussion Forum and compare them with what your fellow participants have noted.

We will come back to your and your fellow participants' reflections later on.

Learning about other active citizens will be a useful starting point to understand the diversity of their backgrounds, their character, and the different roles they play as active citizens. Therefore, this section contains stories of a number of active citizens highlighting the diverse nature of active citizens, some providing an in-depth insight into particular aspects of active citizens. While reading these stories you are encouraged to give some thought to this diversity.

Happy reading!
