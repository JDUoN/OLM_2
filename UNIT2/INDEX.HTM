Theme 2 - Who is an Active Citizen?

In Theme 2 we are going to explore who active citizens are. In particular we will try to identify and appreciate:

  • the diverse backgrounds of active citizens

  • the different experiences they undergo in becoming active citizens

  • the variety of roles they play as active citizens, and

  • the common characteristics (if there are any) that can be found among active citizens

We are going to explore these issues through the life stories of a number of active citizens whom we have met in our work. We also want you to draw on your own experience as an active citizen or/and as an educator.
