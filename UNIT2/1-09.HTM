The report on the Life History Research

Click on the above title and follow the links to download the report (as separate chapters) in .PDF format.

This report, an outcome of a major empirical work within the Etgace project, explores how adults learn to become active citizens. You will be able to read profiles of over 100 active citizens from the six countries that took part in the project.

The following sections of the report may be helpful for you to learn about a diverse group of active citizens from across the six countries in Europe.
