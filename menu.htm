This on-line learning material has been produced as part of a European-wide research project investigating how adults learn to become active citizens and how such learning processes can be encouraged.

The full title of the research project is: 'Education and Training for Governance and Active Citizenship in Europe (ETGACE): Analysis of Adult Learning and Design of Formal, Non-Formal and Informal Educational Intervention Strategies'. More details of the project can be found at; www.surrey.ac.uk/education/etgace

The ETGACE project has given rise to new perspectives on active citizenship, and how it is learnt and could be facilitated. The aim of the learning material is to share those new perspectives with the educator community, who play a major role in facilitating, fostering, and educating for active citizenship.
