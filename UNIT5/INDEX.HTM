Theme 5 - Education for Active Citizenship and Governance

In Theme 5 we are going to explore how we can strengthen the learning for active citizenship and governance. We'll look at:

  • the various educational approaches and strategies available for encouraging active citizenship and governance in various European countries

  • the main features of those programmes

  • their achievements and outcomes, and

  • how these approaches can strengthen the process of becoming an active citizen.

We will explore these issues through brief descriptions of a number of learning and community development programmes. We also invite you to draw on your own experience as an active citizen or/and as an educator.
