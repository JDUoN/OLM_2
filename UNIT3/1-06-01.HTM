Our colleagues from other volunteer and community organisations also recognise these tendencies in relation to volunteering activities. Students are often burdened with very heavy requirements in their study and in their work. They are often treated as full employees and many of them work very long hours.

While the level of participation of youth and students is becoming a cause for some concern, participation of the elderly population probably increases. Not just because of the increase of the category of senior citizens in society, but also because the new generations of elderly are better educated, emancipated and independent than former generations.

Joël van Raak & Ruud van der Veen
